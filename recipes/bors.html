Bors Recipe

Bors recipe step by step guide

Bors is a traditional Eastern European soup that is particularly popular in countries such as Russia, Ukraine, Poland, and other neighboring regions, including Romania and Moldova. It is known for its distinctive sour taste, which is achieved through the fermentation of beets or other souring agents. There are various regional variations of bors, and it can be served hot or cold.
