Mamaliga Recipe

Mamaliga recipe step by step guide

Mămăligă is a traditional and popular dish in Romania and Moldova, as well as in other Eastern European countries. It is a type of cornmeal porridge or polenta that has been a staple in the region for centuries. Mămăligă holds a special place in the culinary culture of these countries and is often considered a comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Salt
  3. Gradually Add Cornmeal
  4. Cook and Stir
  5. Reduce Heat
  6. Simmer and Stir
  7. Check for Consistency
  8. Mold or Shape
  9. Serve
